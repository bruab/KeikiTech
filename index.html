Junior Game Development Workshop this month!

  • When: Friday, June 27 (3-7pm) and Saturday, June 28 (10am-4pm)
  • Where: UH Hilo CH 11 (campus map here, homemade map here)
  • Who's invited: Aspiring computer scientists and game developers ages 8-16
  • Instructors: Brian and Teddy

Interested in game programming? Come join us the last weekend in June. We'll demonstrate the basic concepts and components that go into video game design, and students will have a chance to get their hands dirty writing real code. At the end of the weekend, participants will leave with a basic game that they helped make -- and can add to on their own.
